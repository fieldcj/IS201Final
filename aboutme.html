About Me

My passions, interests and family-life

Who am I?

Hello! My name is Colby Field and it is a pleasure to introduce myself to you. :)

I am a 21 year old from Highlands Ranch, Colorado and one of 6 people in my amazing family. My dad and mom, an older sister and a brother-in-law, and a twin brother. We love to watch movies, play MarioKart, and eat at Chuck-A-Rama together!

Our first family photo in many years!

I am currently studying at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah as a sophomore studying Pre-Business. I hope to one day study Accounting in the infamous accounting program here! I have also enjoyed learning about a variety of other topics here such as art history, dance, and religious topics.

I just recently returned home from serving a mission for my church in Bogota, Colombia earlier this year. I absolutely loved the opportunity to meet new people, explore a new culture, and to learn a new language as well as share my beliefs about God's love with others.

I am a social butterfly and really love to meet new people and have new experiences! This year, I was able to go to a young singles adult convention where artists OneRepublic, Jordin Sparks, and Jordan Smith sang, as well as many rooms and social events to meet others and to learn more about God. I had a lot of fun and got to meet so many new friends!

I also love to work hard and have fun! This past summer, I got to work as a youth camp counselor for the program "For the Strength of Youth" and loved every minute of it! Click here if you want to learn more about the program and what it does for youth!
